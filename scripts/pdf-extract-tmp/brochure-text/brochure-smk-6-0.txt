National Institute of Technology Hamirpur (NIT Hamirpur), An Institute of National Importance, is one of Bharat’s leading institutions of higher technical education, located in the serene and picturesque landscape of Himachal Pradesh. Established in 1986 as a Regional Engineering College (REC), it was elevated to the status of a National Institute of Technology in 2002 and recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of Bharat. Spread over 177 acres, the campus of NIT Hamirpur is known for its calm environment, lush greenery, and the inspiring backdrop of the Himalayan hills. This unique setting offers students not only high-quality education but also a peaceful and motivating atmosphere for academic growth and research. The institute offers a wide range of programs including B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, MSc, B.Arch, M.Arch, and PhD, covering engineering, architecture, sciences, management, and humanities. NIT Hamirpur is renowned for its strong academic curriculum, outcome-based learning, modern laboratories, central library, high-speed digital facilities, and a vibrant research ecosystem. The faculty comprises experienced academicians, researchers, and scientists committed to mentoring and shaping future innovators. The Department of Holistic Education (DHE) was established to advance value-based, skill-oriented, and innovation-driven education aligned with the National Education Policy 2020. Inspired by the philosophy of Vidya Bharti, DHE works to nurture responsible citizens, creative thinkers, and ethical leaders by integrating Bharatiya values, modern knowledge, and experiential learning. Founded in 2021 under Vidya Bharti Punjab and scaled nationally in 2023 under Vidya Bharti Institute of Training and Research Trust. DHE today functions as a global platform for educational reform, leadership development,   and   research   and   innovation   in education.  Event Management Cell – Organizing Impactful Events  The Event Management Cell is one of the 25 dynamic working cells of DHE. It is a dedicated operational unit responsible for planning, coordinating, and executing academic, cultural, and large-scale institutional events with excellence and precision, ensuring seamless experiences and impactful engagement at all levels. Shiksha Mahakumbh Abhiyan (SMA) is the fulcrum of this Cell. SHIKSHA MAHAKUMBH ABHIYAN  Shiksha Mahakumbh Abhiyan is a visionary, multi-edition, multinational, and multidimensional movement conceptualized by a distinguished ISRO scientist and eminent author, and shaped under the guidance of visionary educationists and social reformers. The initiative brings together education, policy, industry, civil society, and youth on a shared platform to reimagine and strengthen the Bhartiya education system while positioning Bharat as a meaningful contributor to the global education discourse. Rooted in Bharatiya knowledge traditions and aligned with global educational contexts, the Abhiyan serves as a powerful platform for collaboration, innovation, and implementation. Its aspiration goes beyond national boundaries—envisioning education on a global stage, much like the Olympic spirit—where local experiences and global perspectives converge to shape the future of learning. At its core lies a pragmatic yet forward-looking vision: to build a global education ecosystem that is inclusive, interdisciplinary, ethical, and transformation-driven—deeply connected with the real needs of society. Each edition of the Mahakumbh extends beyond dialogue and deliberation. It generates concrete action plans, fosters institutional partnerships, and encourages community participation, ensuring sustainable and measurable impact. A distinctive strength of the Shiksha Mahakumbh Abhiyan is its Whole-of-Society approach, transcending academic boundaries to engage teachers, students, institutional leaders, policymakers, industry representatives, social organizations, and media in a unified mission. By positioning education as a catalyst for societal transformation, the movement calls for advancing with national consciousness and global responsibility. Each edition builds upon the achievements of the previous one, creating a continuous chain of innovation and implementation. So far SMA has a legacy   of 5 editions held at   NIT Jalandhar, NIT Kurukshetra, NIT Srinagar, Kurukshetra University and NIPER Mohali.  A People’s Movement for Global Educational Transformation
